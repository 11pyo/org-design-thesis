**Publication 1:**
- **Title:** Adaptive Limits of Algorithmic Cryptocurrency Trading from a Complex Adaptive Systems Perspective: An Empirical Study on Human-AI Collaborative Strategy
- **Publication / publisher:** SSRN
- **Publication date:** 2026-03-27 (Submitted) / 2026-04-28 (Posted)
- **Publication URL:** `https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=6477100`
- **Description:**
  ```
  Sole-authored. Empirical study testing why composite technical trading algorithms struggle
  in cryptocurrency markets, framed through Complex Adaptive Systems theory. 160+ backtesting
  experiments across 10 cryptocurrency assets over 45 months. Six complementary analyses:
  walk-forward cross-validation, Q-Learning reinforcement learning, sentiment integration,

  algorithm only during detected downtrends significantly outperforms always-on deployment
  ```

## 6. 게시물(Post) — 권장하지 않음

LinkedIn 피드에 "내 논문 봐주세요" 게시는 **하지 말 것**:
- 학술계 일반 인식: 자기 홍보 게시는 oversell로 비침
- 논문이 막 등록된 단계라 동료심사 통과 전 — pop framing 위험
- 차라리 Featured/Publications 섹션에 조용히 등록 → 채용/네트워킹 시 자연스럽게 노출됨

페이퍼가 peer-review 통과하거나 큰 미디어 인용 이후에는 한 번 정도 post 가능.
