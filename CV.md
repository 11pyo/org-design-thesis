**Han, W.** (2026). *Adaptive Limits of Algorithmic Cryptocurrency Trading from a Complex Adaptive Systems Perspective: An Empirical Study on Human-AI Collaborative Strategy.* SSRN Working Paper No. 6477100. (Posted.)

- Six complementary analyses: walk-forward cross-validation, Q-Learning RL, sentiment integration, outlier robustness, fee sensitivity, real-time regime detection
